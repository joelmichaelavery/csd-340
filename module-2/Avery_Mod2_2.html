The Biography of Joel Avery

Chapter I

It all began in a small town outside of Houston Texas, in a little town called Porter, Texas. I grew up with a moderate sized family. A set of hard-working parents that made some bad decisions. They loved with all their hearts, but little did they know their kindness would be their weakness.

My parents were never really very good with managing money, and the sad part is if someone needed a hand they would always help. Eventually this led to issues with my family getting taken advantage of and sometimes there would not even be food on the table. This was not a great time, as my parents did everything they could to take care of us. They were such hard workers it was just a challenge to see them hurting.

As I got older I followed the same traits that they did. For instance, when I was a kid growing up, I remember my parents checking the mail. They would say bill, and proceed to throw it in the trash. The issue with this was as a child, that is what I knew. I still had a love for helping people, but I had some bad traits that stemmed from my childhood. It wasn't until my buddy Wayne said, "What are you doing with your life?" This was a wakeup call.

It was this question that awoke something in my brain that made me think, maybe I am not making the best decisions. From this point I began talking to people, reading articles to help my 400 credit score. I knew that I had a long way to go. Even though I knew it was going to be a challenge, I wanted to be the one to break the cycle. How could I still help people while helping myself?
